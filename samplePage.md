# Accomplishment

1. Usually refers to accomplishment for spiritual practices.
指修行上的成就

- Chinese: 成就
- Tibetan: དངོས་གྲུབ་

See also: siddhi, 悉地

Example:
- "he [Milarepa] gained the supreme accomplishment within that very lifetime" from http://www.rigpawiki.org/index.php?title=Milarepa
